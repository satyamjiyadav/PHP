Options:
  • A. 57
  • B. 59
  • C. 61
  • D. 63
Correct Answer: A
Explanation: Since, the object is symmetric so we have to count the additional cubes in each layer.
The number of cubes in first layer = 5
Now, in second layer there are 8 additional cubes. So, number of cubes in second layer = 5 + 8 = 13
In third layer, there are again 8 additional cubes. So, number of cubes in third layer = 13 + 8 = 21
Since, figure being symmetric
Thus, the fourth layer is symmetric to second layer. So, number of cubes in fourth layer = 13
Also, the fifth layer is symmetric to first ayer. So, number of cubes in fifth layer = 5
So, total number of cubes = 5 + 13 + 21 + 13 + 5 = 57

                           +  D E E P
                           +  B E E P
                               L  I  P S

Value of D?
Options:
  • A. 1
  • B. 1 or 5
  • C. 5
  • D. 6
Correct Answer: B
Explanation:                                P E E P
                               D E E P
                           +  B E E P
                               L  I  P S
 
Lets start with the units place. If we put P =0, we will get S=0, which is not possible as each letter has a distinct value.
 
If we put P =1 then S=3, Now  when P =1 then E = 7, because 7+7+7 will end
with a 1.  So now we will get :
 
 
                               1 7 7 1
                               D 7 7 1
                           +  B 7 7 1
                               L  I  1 3
Now at the hundreds place there will be a carry over of 2, because of which we will get I=3(7+7+7+2=23) , Now both I=S=3 which is not possible. So P=1,this case is invalid
Now if we put P=2 then S=6, Now when P=2 then E=4, because 4+4+4 will end with a 2. So now we will get:
 
                               2 4 4 2
                               D 4 4 2
                           +  B 4 4 2
                               L  I  2 6
 
Now at the hundreds place there will be a carry over of 1, because of which we will get I= 3(4+4+4+1). Now when I =3 we will get:
                               2 4 4 2
                               D 4 4 2
                           +  B 4 4 2
                               L  3 2 6
Now we have used digits 2,3,4,6 and now 0 cannot be used as a number cannot start with a 0. We are left with 3 variables and the possible digits to be used are 1,5,7,8 and 9.  Now as  L cannot be greater than 9, so the sum of
B+D cannot be greater than 6 because there is already a carry over of 1 at the thousands place. So neither B nor D can be 7,8,or 9. So the only possibilities for B and D are 1 and 5 and which will give L=9
 
So here are the values :
P=2  ,E=4,  B=1/5,  D=1/5 ,  L=9,   I=3, and S=6
 
 
                               2   4 4 2
                             1/5 4 4 2
                           +5/1 4 4 2
                               9  3 2 6
Value for D is either 1 or 5

    131416
  • B.
    232826
  • C.
    121913
  • D.
    242726
Correct Answer: C

If E = 2, then N = 6 and V = 8.
For ELEVEN to be divisible by 11, ELEVEN must be 121913 or 232826.
232826.is eliminated because even T = 9 can't yield 23xxxx.
The only possible combinations of R and I are (0, 8), (4, 0), (5,8) and (7, 4).
(7, 4) is the only combination which yields the value of T = 5, H = 6 and F = 8
 
ELEVEN = 121913.
